Alexander Collin, Studio Collin

Studio Collin is a creative studio working with creative- & visual direction for artists & brands.

Alexander Collin works interdisciplinary and with a curious nerve to bring forth and execute strong visual concepts. Since founding Studio Collin, he has been creating full visual concepts, artworks, photographs, music videos and 360 campaigns for brands and artists internationally.

The work has been featured and awarded (D&DA, the FWA, Adobe, Behance..), multi-certified as gold and platinum records by IFPI Sweden and published in magazines (Elle Magazine, Kaltblut..)
